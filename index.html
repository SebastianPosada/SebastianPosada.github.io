About myself

Welcome to my game developer portfolio! My name is Sebastian Posada, and I am a passionate game developer based in Medellin, Colombia. With 5 years of experience, I specialize in programming functionalities and systems using Unreal Engine 5. My expertise lies in developing gameplay features, implementing multiplayer infrastructure, and integrating web technologies within game environments.

Medellin Fun City

Medellin Fun City, developed by Sparklife, aimed to provide an immersive experience for local businesses in Medellin. I contributed to the development of this project by implementing gameplay mechanics, optimizing performance, and ensuring seamless multiplayer interactions. My role also involved coordinating the team to deliver high-quality results.

Astroverse

Astroverse, a Play-to-earn game based on the BSC blockchain with its own token and array of impactful gameplay-changing NFT's, a third person survival, an astronaut traveling through a solar system searching for the runes necessary to restore the enery balance in his home planet

Cannaverse

Cannaverse is a cutting-edge metaverse owned by Bancannabis. It combines the power of Unreal Engine 5 with blockchain technology to create a virtual world where participants from the Cannabis industry can connect, socialize, and conduct business. As a developer on this project, I played a key role in designing and implementing the multiplayer infrastructure and programming the core functionalities.
